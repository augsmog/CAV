VALUATION MODEL COMPONENTS
==========================

1. Performance Calculator (models/performance.py)
   - Position-specific metrics
   - WAR (Wins Above Replacement)
   - Conference adjustments
   - Advanced statistics

2. Scheme Fit Calculator (models/scheme_fit.py)
   - Air Raid, Spread, Pro Style, Option, West Coast
   - Physical/skill requirements matching
   - Learning curve estimation
   - Archetype compatibility

3. Brand Valuation Calculator (models/brand_valuation.py)
   - Social media quantification
   - NIL value estimation
   - Market size factors
   - Marketability scoring

4. Valuation Engine (models/valuation_engine.py)
   - Integrates all components
   - Produces final valuations
   - Transfer recommendations
   - Risk assessment
